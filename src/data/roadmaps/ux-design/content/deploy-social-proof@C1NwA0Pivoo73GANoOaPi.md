# Deploy Social Proof

Social proof shows users that other people have used, trusted, or approved of a product, through reviews, testimonials, user counts, or ratings. People tend to look to others' behavior when they are uncertain about a decision, so visible social proof reduces hesitation. It is commonly placed near decision points like signup forms or purchase buttons.
