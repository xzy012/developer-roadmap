# Persuasive Technology

Persuasive technology refers to digital products deliberately designed to change a user's attitude or behavior through interaction, not through argument or coercion. Fitness apps that reward streaks or apps that use progress bars to keep users engaged are common examples. The field studies which design elements make a system convincing and how far that influence should go before it becomes manipulative.
