# Human Decision Making

Human decision-making encompasses the cognitive processes and influences that drive individuals to select a course of action from multiple options. It involves evaluating information, considering biases, weighing potential outcomes, and ultimately choosing what they believe is the best path forward in a given situation. Understanding how people make decisions is crucial for creating user experiences that align with their natural tendencies and help them achieve their goals effectively.
