# Customer Experience Map by Mel Edwards

The Customer Experience Map is a deliverable developed by Mel Edwards that visualizes a user's journey through a product across stages, capturing their actions, thoughts, and emotions at each point. It highlights pain points and moments of friction alongside points of delight across the full experience. Teams use it during conceptual design to see the product from the user's perspective before deciding what to build or fix.
