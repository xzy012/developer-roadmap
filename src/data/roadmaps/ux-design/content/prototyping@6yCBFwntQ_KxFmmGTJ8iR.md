# Prototyping

Prototyping is the stage where a design idea is turned into a testable representation, ranging from low-fidelity wireframes to interactive high-fidelity mockups. It lets designers and stakeholders evaluate layout, flow, and interaction before development begins. Tools like Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD are commonly used to build prototypes at this stage.
