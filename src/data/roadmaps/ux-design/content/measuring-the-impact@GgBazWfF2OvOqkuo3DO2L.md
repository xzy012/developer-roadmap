# Measuring the Impact

Measuring the impact is the stage where a team evaluates whether the design decisions made throughout the product actually produced the intended behavior change. It involves testing methods such as multivariate testing and incremental A/B testing to compare outcomes across different design variations. Findings from this stage feed back into earlier design decisions to refine and improve the product.
