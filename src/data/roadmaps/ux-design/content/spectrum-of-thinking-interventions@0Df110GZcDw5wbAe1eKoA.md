# Spectrum of Thinking Interventions

The spectrum of thinking interventions ranges from automatic, low-effort nudges on one end to interventions that demand deliberate, conscious thought on the other. Design choices sit somewhere along this spectrum depending on how much reflection the target behavior needs. Choosing the wrong point on the spectrum, such as an automatic nudge for a decision that needs careful thought, can backfire.
