# Competitor Analysis

Competitor analysis is the process of researching other products or businesses that solve a similar problem for similar users. It covers their features, pricing, positioning, and user experience to find gaps and opportunities for differentiation. Teams use it before designing a new business model or product feature so decisions are informed rather than made in isolation.
