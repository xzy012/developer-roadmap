# SWOT Analysis

SWOT analysis evaluates a business or product across four categories: strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Strengths and weaknesses look inward at the organization itself, while opportunities and threats look outward at the market and competition. Teams use it as a quick way to structure a strategic discussion before deciding on a new business direction.
