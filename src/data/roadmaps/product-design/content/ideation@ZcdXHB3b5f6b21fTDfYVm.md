# Ideation

Ideation is the stage where a team generates a wide range of possible solutions to a defined problem before narrowing down to the best ones. It includes techniques like brainstorming, mind mapping, sketching, wireframing, and early prototyping. The goal at this stage is breadth of ideas first, with evaluation and refinement happening afterward.
