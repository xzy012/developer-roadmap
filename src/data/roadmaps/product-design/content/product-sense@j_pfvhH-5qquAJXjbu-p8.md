# Product Sense

Product sense is a designer's or product manager's ability to judge what will genuinely work well for users and the business, built from experience and pattern recognition rather than a fixed process. It helps teams make fast, reasonable calls in situations where full research or data is not available. While useful, product sense is often paired with structured frameworks and user feedback to check for blind spots.
