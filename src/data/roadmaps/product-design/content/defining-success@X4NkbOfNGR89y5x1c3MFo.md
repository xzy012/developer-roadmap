# Defining Success

Defining success is the early step of setting clear goals, a vision, and measurable outcomes for a product before design work begins. It includes clarifying the product vision, value proposition, and the metrics that will indicate whether the product is working. Without this step, teams risk building features that look good but do not move any meaningful outcome.
