# Product Design vs UX

Product design covers the full lifecycle of a product, including strategy, business alignment, and shipping decisions, while UX design focuses more narrowly on how a user experiences and interacts with a product. A product designer often makes calls on what to build and why, not only how it should look or feel. The two roles overlap heavily in practice, and many companies use the titles interchangeably.
