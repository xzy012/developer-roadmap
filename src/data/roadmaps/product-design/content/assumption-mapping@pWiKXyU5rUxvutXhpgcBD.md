# Assumption Mapping

Assumption mapping is the practice of listing out the underlying beliefs a product idea depends on, then ranking them by how risky and how uncertain each one is. It helps a team see which assumptions need to be tested before moving forward, rather than treating all beliefs as equally solid. This step often produces a shortlist of experiments needed to validate the riskiest assumptions first.
