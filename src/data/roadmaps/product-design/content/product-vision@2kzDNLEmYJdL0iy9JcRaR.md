# Product Vision

A product vision is a concise statement of what a product aims to become and why it matters, usually looking several years ahead. It gives a team a consistent direction to align decisions against, even as individual features and priorities shift over time. A strong vision is specific enough to guide choices but broad enough to survive changes in strategy.
