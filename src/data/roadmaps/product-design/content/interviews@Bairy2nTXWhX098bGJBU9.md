# Interviews

Interviews are one-on-one conversations with users designed to uncover their needs, motivations, and pain points in their own words. They allow a researcher to ask follow-up questions and dig deeper into unexpected answers, which surveys cannot do. Interviews are typically used earlier in a research process to generate insights that other methods can later validate at scale.
