# User-Centered Design

User-centered design is an approach that puts real user needs, behaviors, and feedback at the center of every design decision, rather than starting from technical constraints or internal assumptions. It typically involves ongoing research, testing, and iteration based on how actual users interact with a product. This principle underlies most of the research and validation activities in a product design process.
