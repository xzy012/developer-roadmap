# Value Proposition

A value proposition is a clear statement of the specific benefit a product delivers to its users and why it is better than existing alternatives. It answers the question of why someone would choose this product over doing nothing or using a competitor. Product teams use it to keep design and marketing decisions focused on the core value being delivered.
