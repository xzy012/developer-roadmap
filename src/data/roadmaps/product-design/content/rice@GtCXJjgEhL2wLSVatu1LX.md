# RICE

RICE is a prioritization framework that scores potential projects based on four factors: reach, impact, confidence, and effort, combined into a single comparable score. Reach and impact estimate how many users are affected and how much, confidence accounts for uncertainty in those estimates, and effort represents the cost of building it. Teams use the resulting score to rank features or projects on a consistent, numeric basis.
