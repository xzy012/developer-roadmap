# Prototyping

Prototyping is the process of building an interactive or semi-interactive representation of a design so it can be tested and reviewed before actual development begins. Prototypes range from simple clickable wireframes to detailed, high-fidelity simulations of the final product. They let designers and stakeholders evaluate flow and interaction in a way that static images cannot fully convey.
