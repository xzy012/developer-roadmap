# Business Alignment

Business alignment is the process of connecting design and product decisions to the broader goals, strategy, and metrics of the business. It includes understanding how the business operates, shaping product strategy, and tracking relevant business metrics and OKRs. Without this alignment, product design risks producing work that pleases users but does not support the company's underlying objectives.
