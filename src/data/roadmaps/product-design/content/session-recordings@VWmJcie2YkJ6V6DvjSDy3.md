# Session Recordings

Session recordings capture a real user's interactions with a product, such as mouse movement, clicks, and scrolling, played back as a video. They let designers observe exactly where users hesitate, get confused, or abandon a task. Unlike interviews, they show actual behavior rather than what users say they do.
