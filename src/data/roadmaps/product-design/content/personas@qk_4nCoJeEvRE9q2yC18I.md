# Personas

Personas are fictional but research-based profiles representing key types of users a product serves, built from patterns found in real research rather than assumptions. Each persona typically includes goals, frustrations, and behaviors relevant to the product being designed. Teams reference personas throughout design and prioritization to keep decisions grounded in actual user needs.
