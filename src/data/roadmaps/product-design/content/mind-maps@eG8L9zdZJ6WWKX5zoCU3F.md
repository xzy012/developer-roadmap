# Mind Maps

A mind map is a diagram that visually branches out from a central topic into related ideas, themes, or subtopics. It helps a team explore connections between ideas that might not be obvious when listed in a straight line. Mind maps are commonly used early in ideation to organize a broad brainstorm into related clusters.
