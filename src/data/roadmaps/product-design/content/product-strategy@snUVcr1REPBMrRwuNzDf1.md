# Product Strategy

Product strategy is the high-level plan for how a product will achieve its vision and business goals over time, including what to build, for whom, and in what order. It connects the product vision to concrete decisions about focus areas and trade-offs. Design decisions at every later stage are typically expected to support this strategy rather than conflict with it.
