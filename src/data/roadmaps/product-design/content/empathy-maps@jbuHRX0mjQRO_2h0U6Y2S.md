# Empathy Maps

An empathy map is a simple diagram that captures what a user says, thinks, does, and feels regarding a specific situation or product. It helps a team step into the user's perspective and surface needs or frustrations that may not be explicitly stated. Empathy maps are often built directly from interview or observation data gathered earlier in the research process.
