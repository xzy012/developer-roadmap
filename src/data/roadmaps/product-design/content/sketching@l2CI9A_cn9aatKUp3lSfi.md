# Sketching

Sketching is the practice of quickly drawing rough visual representations of an idea, often by hand, to explore layout and interaction concepts before committing to a digital tool. Its low fidelity makes it fast to produce and easy to discard, which encourages exploring many directions rather than over-investing in one idea too early. Sketches typically feed into more refined wireframes once a direction starts to take shape.
