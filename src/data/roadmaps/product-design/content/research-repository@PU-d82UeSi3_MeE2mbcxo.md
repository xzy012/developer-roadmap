# Research Repository

A research repository is a centralized, searchable store of past research findings, recordings, and insights that a team can reference over time. It prevents duplicate research efforts and makes it easier for new team members to catch up on what is already known about users. Well-maintained repositories are usually tagged and organized by topic, project, or user segment.
