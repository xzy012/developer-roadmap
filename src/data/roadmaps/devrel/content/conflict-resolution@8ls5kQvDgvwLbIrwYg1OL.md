# Conflict Resolution
 
Conflict resolution addresses disputes between community members before they damage the community's health. This involves understanding each party's perspective, applying the community guidelines consistently, and helping members reach a resolution or enforcing consequences when necessary. Handled well, conflict resolution demonstrates that the community is safe and governed fairly.
