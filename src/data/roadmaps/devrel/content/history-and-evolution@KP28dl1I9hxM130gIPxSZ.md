# History and Evolution
 
DevRel as a formal discipline emerged in the late 1980s when Apple and Sun Microsystems created dedicated teams to support third-party developers building on their platforms. Over the following decades it evolved from a support function into a strategic role, growing alongside the API economy and open-source movement. Today it covers a broad range of activities from technical content creation to community building and product feedback.
