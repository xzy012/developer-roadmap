# Conference Speaking
 
Conference speaking is one of the highest-visibility activities in DevRel. A well-received talk at a major developer conference can reach thousands of developers and build lasting credibility. Building a speaking career involves developing signature talks, submitting to CFPs (Calls for Papers), and investing in the preparation and delivery skills that make talks memorable.
